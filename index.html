How to Order

  1. Choose anything from the menu.
  2. When ready, proceed to checkout.
  3. Give us your delivery adress (and billing adress, if it is not the same).
  4. Pay with credit card (you will be transferred to a secure page). No cash payment is accepted.
  5. Wait for delivery.
  6. Enjoy your meal.
  7. Repeat, if you like.
